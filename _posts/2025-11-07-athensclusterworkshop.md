We are proud to share that the TrialsNet Athens live demonstration event was successfully held on November 5th, 2025, at the **Sofitel Athens Airport**.
{: .text-justify}

The event, organized by the **Athens International Airport (AIA)**, the **Municipal IT Development Agency of the City of Athens (DAEM)**, and **WINGS ICT Solutions**, showcased cutting-edge 5G, Beyond 5G (B5G), and 6G connectivity solutions applied in real-life scenarios — from smart city infrastructure to airport automation and emergency response.
{: .text-justify}

Participants had the opportunity to experience:
- Predictive asset management for public infrastructure (AIA–DAEM–WINGS)
- Autonomous apron operations (AIA–WINGS)
- Emergency rescue and mass casualty incident management (WINGS–DAEM)
- Passenger service robots (AIA–WINGS)
- XR museum experiences (WINGS)
- 5G-enabled secure surveillance systems (Zelus)

A big thank you to all participants, partners, and attendees who made this event a success!
{: .text-justify}

Together, we are paving the way toward a **smarter, safer, and more connected future**.
{: .text-justify}
