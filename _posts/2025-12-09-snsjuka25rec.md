As you know, the TrialsNet project was honored to be selected as one of the 2025 Top-10 Key Achievements of the Smart Networks and Services Joint Undertaking (SNS JU). An SNS JA webinar, “From Research to Impact: Exploring the Top-10 Key Achievements of SNS JU Projects,” has been organized to serve as the official presentation ceremony for all the most significant, innovative, and influential results emerging from the SNS JU community. If you missed the live session on December 3rd, you can now catch up! You have the opportunity to revisit the entire session and, specifically, the presentation detailing TrialsNet's exceptional achievement: “Advanced XR Capabilities over B5G during Live Events @26 GHz” (1:11:16). The recording is available [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWSTPhXZ_P0)
{: .text-justify}

You can also explore the Top-10 Key Achievements showcased and revisit the details of TrialsNet's success in the SNS Key Achievements Repository, [here](https://smart-networks.europa.eu/sns-ju-projects-key-achievements-2025/?utm_source=mailpoet&utm_medium=email&utm_source_platform=mailpoet)
